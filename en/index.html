OUR SERVICES

From the heart of the workshop to the depths of the mine, at REDIMAK we solve with precision what others merely attempt to repair. Each service we offer is backed by years of experience, genuine commitment, and specialized technical attention.

Specialized Diagnostics

Before taking action, we listen to your machinery. Our technicians perform in-depth inspections to detect issues, prevent major failures, and provide accurate solutions from the very first visit.

Heavy Machinery Repair

We get your operation up and running. We repair industrial and mining equipment with a focus on performance, safety, and durability—whether on-site inside the mine or at the mine’s maintenance workshop, depending on what the job requires.

Preventive & Corrective Maintenance

Downtime is not an option. We design custom maintenance plans to extend the lifespan of your assets and reduce unexpected breakdowns.

Machinery & Supplies Sales

Reliable equipment. Guaranteed supply. We offer verified used machinery, industrial tools, parts, and consumables selected by technicians—not salespeople
